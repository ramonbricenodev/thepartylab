Wedding Kids

At ThePartyLab we understand that weddings are not just about celebrating love: they're about creating unforgettable memories for everyone, including the littlest members of your family. Look no further, because we’ve got you covered, so you can enjoy your special day to the fullest.

Keep the little ones entertained

While you're sipping champagne and sharing moments with friends and family, we provide a kids’ station complete with elegant low white or wooden tables, tipi tents, plush pillows, rugs, an array of toys and engaging activities—all supervised by our trusted, experienced “Super Nannies.”

Super Nannies

Our experienced and loving nannies provide engaging, safe, and fun care throughout your wedding. From games to quiet time, we ensure every child stays happy and entertained for the entire celebration.

A Safe Place to Play

Designed with safety in mind, our Soft Play areas and bouncy castles let kids burn off energy, laugh, and play—all while staying close to the festivities. Turn your wedding into the family-friendly event everyone will remember.

Concessions

From dreamy ice-cream bars to candy-floss clouds and popcorn stands, we bring the fun with nostalgic treats that kids (and grown-ups!) will love. Sweet smiles guaranteed!
