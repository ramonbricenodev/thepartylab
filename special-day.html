Wedding Planning & Coordination

As a couple planning a wedding, you’ve got a lot on your plate. From choosing a list of special guests to celebrating with, to spending probably the most you ever have so far in life, it can be a whirlwind—and that’s where we step in!

Through knowledge and support, our passionate team empowers couples to plan their Mallorca weddings with confidence. Each planning and coordination experience is tailored to meet the unique needs, wants, and visions of our clients, curating seamless and positive wedding journeys (almost) as special as the destination!

Whether you want us along for every step of your planning journey, need us to jump in on the day of to coordinate, or are looking for something in between, we would be honoured to be a part of your special day. We love weddings of all shapes, sizes, and levels of support. We understand each celebration is exceptional and calls for varying extents of assistance.
