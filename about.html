It all begins with an idea

Lidia the Bruijn, the founder of ThePartyLab, started her journey into the world of entertainment with a deep passion for creativity and musical theater. She studied at DAPA (Dutch Academy Of Performing Arts), where she discovered her love for performing, creating and exploring her entrepreneurship into entertainment.

After many years of experience in children's entertainment with her first company KidsPartyLabMallorca with over 300 events , Lidia has evolved the company, steering it in an exciting new direction. Expanding her services to cater not only to children but also to teenagers and adults, she now offers events for a wider audience.

Today, ThePartyLab offers tailor-made events for children, teenagers, and adults alike, delivering extraordinary celebrations for every occasion with many services such as entertainment, activities, decoration, catering and live music! Driven by creativity, enthusiasm, and a shared passion for bringing people together, ThePartyLab team is here to turn your special moments into lifelong memories.
